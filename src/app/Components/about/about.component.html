About me

I'm currently attending the University of Pittsburgh. I spend much of my free time writing stories or creating programming projects. I created this website to hold some of my stories and projects, and a little bit about me. Now, I have a place that is mine to post my things; and this is it.

I'm a perfectionist but a chaotic one. My stories must all be perfect in my own head, but my path to get there isn't always straightforward.
